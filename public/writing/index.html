Selected work

A working sample of writing across AI, B2B fintech, financial services and journalism.

I'm a freelance journalist, editor and copywriter. I primarily create content for fintech and financial services clients, covering topics such as payments, anti-money laundering, open banking, wealth management, private equity and regtech. Recently, I led AI evaluation work on contract at Contextual AI and co-authored a research paper on structured document extraction submitted to KDD 2026.

My reporting and editing background is in financial services, focusing on investment banking, capital markets, insurance and legal services at FinanceAsia, American Lawyer, Euromoney and most recently at Neudata, where I edited research reports on alternative data providers for an audience of hedge fund managers.
